About CivicFix

Our mission, vision, and how we’re bringing real change to Solapur.

Mission

CivicFix is designed to give Solapur’s citizens a direct, simple, and transparent way to raise civic issues — whether it’s potholes on bustling roads, overflowing garbage in residential areas, or faulty streetlights. Our mission is to ensure every problem is not only reported but also tracked and resolved efficiently by bridging the gap between people and the local administration.

Vision

We envision a Solapur where civic management is collaborative, tech-driven, and responsive. A city where citizens actively participate in shaping their surroundings, authorities stay accountable, and every small complaint contributes to a larger change. Through CivicFix, we aim to build a cleaner, safer, and more connected Solapur — one issue at a time.
