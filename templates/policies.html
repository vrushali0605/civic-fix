CivicFix - Government Policies & Schemes

Major Government Schemes

Swachh Bharat Abhiyan

A nationwide initiative to improve urban sanitation and eliminate open defecation by encouraging cleanliness and hygiene in public areas.

AMRUT (Atal Mission)

Focuses on urban renewal and transformation through development of infrastructure like water supply, sewerage, urban transport, and green spaces.

Smart Cities Mission

Aims to make cities citizen-friendly and sustainable by promoting smart solutions and better urban planning with e-governance integration.

Jal Jeevan Mission

Seeks to provide safe and adequate drinking water through individual household tap connections to all rural and urban households.
